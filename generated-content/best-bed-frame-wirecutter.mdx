## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Bed Frame Wirecutter

### 1. Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before making a purchase, identify who the bed is for and the specific size of the room. If you have limited space or need a bed for guests, a folding model or a trundle might be the best fit. For a permanent master bedroom setup, consider whether you prefer a minimalist platform or a model with an integrated headboard for better aesthetics and back support when sitting up.

### 2. Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($150-$500)
Quality solid wood bed frames typically range from $150 for twin sizes to $500 for high-end king models with headboards. While you can find cheaper metal frames, they often suffer from noise and durability issues. Investing slightly more in solid hardwood ensures a product that will last for decades rather than years, making the long-term cost per night significantly lower.

### 3. Key Features - Most important technical specifications
Pay close attention to slat spacing. For memory foam and latex mattresses, you need slats that are less than 3 inches apart to prevent sagging and maintain your mattress warranty. Also, check the weight capacity—quality frames should support at least 600 pounds. Finally, ensure the bed is a platform style if you want to avoid the extra expense of a box spring.

### 4. Quality & Durability - What to look for in construction
Kiln-dried hardwood like Tulip Poplar is superior to softwoods or composite materials. The kiln-drying process removes moisture, which prevents the wood from warping, cracking, or squeaking over time. Look for frames that use solid wood slats rather than thin plywood, as this provides a much more stable base for your weight.

### 5. Brand Reputation - Importance of established manufacturers
Buying from a company that manufactures in the USA, such as KD Frames, often guarantees higher quality control and ethical sourcing. Established brands provide better warranties and are more likely to offer support if a part is damaged during shipping.
