## What to Look for Before Buying the Best 1.2 Cordless Impact Drivers

### 1. Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before you make a purchase, consider the primary tasks you will be performing. If you are doing light household repairs or assembling furniture, a 17V or 20V lightweight model with around 1300-1500 in-lbs of torque is ideal. However, for outdoor construction like decking or automotive work, you will want a brushless motor that offers over 2000 in-lbs of torque. Also, check if you already own batteries for a specific brand, as buying a tool-only version can save you significant money.

### 2. Budget - Setting realistic price expectations
You can find quality impact drivers across a wide price range. Budget-friendly kits typically fall between $50 and $90, which usually includes a brushed motor and one battery. Mid-range options with brushless motors and better features cost between $100 and $180. Professional-grade kits from established brands often exceed $200 but offer the best durability, power, and long-term warranty support.

### 3. Key Features - Most important technical specifications
Look for brushless motors as they are more efficient, run cooler, and last longer. Torque is the most critical spec for impact drivers, measured in inch-pounds (in-lbs) or Newton-meters (Nm). Another key feature is the IPM (Impacts Per Minute), which tells you how fast the hammering mechanism works. Finally, ensure the tool has a variable speed trigger and at least two speed settings for better control.

### 4. Quality and Durability - What to look for in construction
A high-quality tool should have a solid housing made of impact-resistant plastic or metal. The chuck should be made of all-metal materials to prevent wear and tear over time. Check for ergonomic rubberized grips that not only provide comfort but also protect the tool from drops. Models with tungsten steel anvils are generally more durable for high-torque applications.

### 5. Brand Reputation - Importance of established manufacturers
Established brands like DEWALT, RYOBI, and BLACK and DECKER offer the peace of mind of extensive service networks and long-term battery availability. However, newer brands like Seesii or GoYwato often offer more features and accessories for a lower price point.
