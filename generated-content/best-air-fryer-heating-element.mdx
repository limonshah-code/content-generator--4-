## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Air Fryer Heating Element

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
The first step is deciding whether you need a full replacement unit or just a spare part. If you are repairing an existing air fryer, check the exact diameter and wattage of your current element. Sizing like the 15.7cm standard is common, but not universal. For a new unit, think about your family size.
