## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Griddle For Smash Burgers

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before you purchase, consider where you will be cooking most often. If you have a large outdoor flat-top griddle like a Blackstone, a heavy cast iron press is almost mandatory. However, if you are cooking indoors on a stainless steel pan or a non-stick skillet, you might prefer a lighter stainless steel press that is easier to clean and wont scratch your surfaces. Also, consider the number of burgers you usually make; a kit with patty papers is essential for high-volume cooking to prevent sticking and mess.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($X-$Y ranges)
You can find high-quality burger tools across a wide price range. A single, reliable cast iron press typically costs between $15 and $25. If you are looking for a comprehensive kit with spatulas, papers, and shakers, expect to spend between $30 and $50. Professional-grade stainless steel options usually fall in the $20 to $35 range. Setting a realistic budget helps you decide if you want one high-quality standalone tool or a complete set of accessories.

### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
The most important features are the diameter and the bottom design. A diameter of 5.5 to 7 inches is standard for most buns. A completely flat bottom is better for ultra-thin smash burgers, while a grooved bottom is better for grill marks and bacon. Heat resistance in the handle is also vital; look for wood or silicone handles to avoid needing gloves.

### Quality & Durability - What to look for in construction
Look for heavy-duty materials. For cast iron, ensure it is either pre-seasoned or has a smooth finish that can be seasoned. For stainless steel, 304 food-grade steel with a thickness of at least 2mm is the standard for durability. Check the handle attachment; dual-screw attachments for wood handles are generally more durable than single-screw versions.

### Brand Reputation - Importance of established manufacturers
