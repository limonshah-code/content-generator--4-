## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Dyson Air Purifier For Pets

### 1. Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before you purchase, decide whether you need a unit for a small bedroom or a large living room. Dyson offers different sizes like the "Cool" tower for large spaces and the "Desk" or "HushJet" for smaller areas. If you have pets that get cold easily, a Hot+Cool model provides both air cleaning and safe warmth.
