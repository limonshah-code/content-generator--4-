## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Bed Frame For Back Pain

### 1. Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
When searching for the Best Bed Frame For Back Pain, you must first determine if you need a standard flat platform or an adjustable base. If you suffer from snoring, acid reflux, or lower lumbar tension, an adjustable base like the Sweetcrispy or Marsail models might be the superior choice. However, if you already have a high-quality orthopedic mattress, a sturdy platform frame with closely spaced slats like the OHOHOLIVE will provide the necessary foundation. Ensure that the frame is compatible with your mattress type, especially since some memory foam mattresses require specific slat spacing to prevent sagging.

### 2. Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($X-$Y ranges)
Bed frames and complete sleep systems vary wildly in price. A high-quality upholstered frame without a mattress typically ranges from $200 to $500.
