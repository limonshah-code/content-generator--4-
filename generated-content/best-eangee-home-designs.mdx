## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Eangee Home Designs

### 1. Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before making a purchase, consider where the lamp will live. If you have high ceilings and a large open space, a 72-inch floor lamp like the Fortune or Wrapped Wave will provide the necessary visual weight. For smaller surfaces like nightstands or floating shelves, the 9-inch mini models are perfect. Think about whether you need the lamp for task lighting, such as reading, or purely for ambient mood lighting. The cylinder models tend to distribute light more evenly for reading, while the decorative shapes are better for mood.

### 2. Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($50-$350 ranges)
Eangee products range from approximately $50 for small accent lamps to over $350 for the large, intricate floor models. Setting a budget helps narrow down your choices between the entry-level mini lamps and the premium giant floor lamps. Keep in mind that these are artisan-made, fair-trade products, so the price reflects the manual labor and sustainable sourcing involved.

### 3. Key Features - Most important technical specifications
