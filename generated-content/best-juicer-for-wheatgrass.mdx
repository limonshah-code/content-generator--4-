## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Juicer For Wheatgrass

### 1. Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before purchasing, consider if you will be juicing wheatgrass exclusively or if you need a machine that handles a variety of produce. Horizontal masticating juicers are traditionally the gold standard for wheatgrass because their longer augers provide a better squeeze for fibrous greens.
