## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Bosch Laser Receivers

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
The first step in choosing a receiver is matching it to your specific laser. Not all receivers work with all lasers. You must determine if you have a rotary laser or a line laser. Furthermore, you need to check if your laser emits a red or green beam. Many modern Bosch receivers are color-specific or designed for either pulsed line lasers or continuous rotary beams. Using the wrong combination will result in no signal at all.
