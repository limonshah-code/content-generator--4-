## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Beautiful Drew Barrymore Microwave

### 1. Find Your Need
Before purchasing, consider if you strictly need a microwave for reheating or if a multi-functional oven might serve you better. If you frequently toast bread or want to air fry, the infrared toaster oven models offer more versatility. However, if your primary goal is quickly heating liquids and leftovers, the 1.1 Cu Ft microwave with sensor technology is the superior choice for speed and simplicity.

### 2. Budget
The Beautiful line typically ranges from $70 to $200 depending on the complexity of the appliance. Simple microwaves and air fryers sit at the lower end of this range, while multi-cookers and infrared ovens represent the premium tier. Set a realistic expectation of around $130 for a high-quality unit that combines style with advanced features like touch displays.

### 3. Key Features
Look for smart sensor cooking if you want to avoid overcooked food. High wattage, typically 1000W for microwaves and 1800W for ovens, ensures fast cooking times. For air fryers, look for specialized technology like TurboCrisp which ensures even air circulation. Touch-activated displays are a signature of this brand and provide a cleaner aesthetic than physical buttons.

### 4. Quality & Durability
Check for metal housing rather than thin plastic, as this ensures the unit can withstand the heat generated over years of use. In bakeware, non-porous stoneware is essential to prevent staining and chipping. The Beautiful line is known for its soft-matte finishes which are durable but may require specific cleaning cloths to maintain their luster without scratching.

### 5. Brand Reputation
The Beautiful collection by Drew Barrymore has established a strong reputation for bringing designer aesthetics to affordable kitchenware. They are manufactured with a focus on ease of use and modern technology. Choosing an established brand ensures you have access to customer support and a community of users who can offer tips and recipes.

### 6. Portability/Size
Measure your countertop space carefully. A 1.1 Cu Ft microwave has a significant footprint, as does a 6-quart air fryer. If you have low-hanging cabinets, the low-profile infrared oven might be the best fit. Consider the clearance needed for heat dissipation, especially with air fryers and toaster ovens that can get quite hot on the exterior.
