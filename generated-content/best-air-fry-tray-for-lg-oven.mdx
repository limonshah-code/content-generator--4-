## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Air Fry Tray For Lg Oven

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
The first step is checking your LG oven model. Some LG ranges have specific side-rail grooves designed for a full-width rack, while others simply require a tray that sits on top of your existing wire racks. If you want a seamless look, search for models that list your specific part number, like LRAL302S. If you have a larger family, prioritize the 18-inch plus models to avoid cooking in multiple batches.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($25-$70 ranges)
Most high-quality air fry trays fall between $25 and $70. The lower end usually includes universal baskets that sit on top of your existing racks. The $50-$70 range typically offers full-sized replacement racks made of 304 stainless steel that slide directly into your oven walls. Investing a bit more often gets you a thicker metal (6mm) that won't warp or 'pop' under high temperatures.

### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
Airflow is king in air frying. Look for a fine mesh basket rather than a solid tray with a few holes. ### Quality & Durability - What to look for in construction
Always prioritize 304 stainless steel. It is food-grade, highly resistant to rust, and handles high heat without releasing toxic fumes. Check the thickness of the frame; a 6mm frame is much more stable than a 4mm frame. Avoid trays with cheap non-stick coatings that can flake off over time, as these flakes can end up in your food.
