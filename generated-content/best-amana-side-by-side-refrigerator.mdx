## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Amana Side By Side Refrigerator

### 1. Find Your Need
Before making a purchase, determine if you need a full appliance or just replacement components. If you have a large family, look for units with at least 20 cu. ft. of space. For those with smaller kitchens, counter-depth models are essential to ensure the fridge does not protrude too far into your walking path. Consider if you prefer a side-by-side for easier freezer access or a top-freezer for a more traditional look.

### 2. Budget
Refrigerators vary significantly in price. A full-sized Amana or Kenmore side-by-side typically ranges from $1,200 to $2,500. If you are just maintaining your current unit, bins and shelves are a much more affordable investment, usually costing between $20 and $60. Setting a realistic expectation for these ranges will help you avoid overspending.

### 3. Key Features
Look for advanced features that improve food preservation. Multi-flow air systems are vital as they circulate air evenly across all shelves. External water and ice dispensers add convenience but require a water line. For maintenance parts, prioritize food-grade materials and reinforced edges, especially for door bins that carry heavy items like milk jugs.

### 4. Quality & Durability
Construction quality is paramount. Check for tempered glass shelves rather than plastic ones, as they can hold more weight and are easier to clean. For the exterior, fingerprint-resistant stainless steel is highly recommended to maintain a clean look. Inverter compressors are another sign of quality, as they are quieter and more energy-efficient than traditional compressors.

### 5. Brand Reputation
Amana, Whirlpool, and Kenmore have established themselves as leaders in the appliance industry for decades. Choosing these brands ensures that finding replacement parts in the future will be easy. Established brands also tend to offer better warranties and have more service technicians available should you ever need a repair.

### 6. Portability/Size
In the world of refrigerators, 'portability' usually refers to how well a unit fits into your specific space. Measure your doorway widths and the depth of your counters multiple times before ordering. Side-by-side models require clearance on both sides for the doors to open fully, so ensure your kitchen layout can accommodate this without hitting walls or other appliances.

## Is it A Wise Decision to Buy amana side by side refrigerator?

Investing in an Amana side-by-side refrigerator is a smart decision for several reasons. First, the side-by-side layout provides exceptional organization, allowing you to keep frequently used items at eye level in both the fridge and freezer sections. This is a significant ergonomic advantage over top or bottom-freezer models where you often have to bend down to reach everyday items. 

Furthermore, Amana appliances are known for their longevity. Unlike some high-tech brands that focus on 'smart' features that can break down easily, Amana tends to focus on robust mechanical systems. This means your investment is likely to last ten to fifteen years with minimal maintenance. While the initial cost may be higher than a budget top-freezer model, the energy savings from modern Energy Star units and the reduction in food waste due to better temperature management make it a financially sound choice in the long run.

## What Is the Best Way to Choose amana side by side refrigerator?

To choose the perfect Amana refrigerator, start by measuring your available space. Height, width, and depth are the three most critical numbers you need. Once you have your dimensions, decide on your storage needs. If you buy in bulk, a larger freezer section is a must. 

Next, compare features like 'Accela Chill' or digital temperature controls. If you are buying replacement parts, the best way to choose is by looking up your specific model number, which is usually found on a sticker inside the refrigerator wall. Do not guess on bin or shelf sizes; use a tape measure to compare your old parts with the listings. Avoid the mistake of choosing purely based on aesthetics; ensure the technical specs like voltage for bulbs or weight limits for shelves meet your household's actual usage patterns.

**What is the best amana side by side refrigerator for beginners?**
For those new to buying large appliances, a mid-sized 20 cu. ft. model with simple digital controls is best. It provides enough space for a growing household without the complexity of smart-home integration which can be confusing. Look for models with pre-installed ice makers to simplify your initial setup.
