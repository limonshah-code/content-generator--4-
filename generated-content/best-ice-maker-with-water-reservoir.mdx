## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Ice Maker With Water Reservoir

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before you purchase, think about how many people will be using the machine daily. If you have a large family or frequently host parties, you will need a unit with at least a 3L reservoir or a water line connection. For solo users or small offices, a 1.5L to 2L tank is usually sufficient for daily hydration without excessive refilling.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($100-$600 ranges)
Setting a budget is crucial as prices vary wildly. Entry-level bullet ice makers with basic reservoirs usually fall in the $100-$150 range.
