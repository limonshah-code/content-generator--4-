## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Microwave Air Fryer Combo

### 1. Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before you hit the buy button, you must determine how you plan to use this appliance. Are you looking for a machine that primarily reheats coffee but can occasionally crisp up some nuggets? Or do you want a full-sized convection oven replacement that can bake cakes and roast chickens? Identifying whether you need a microwave-heavy or an air-fryer-heavy unit will guide you toward either an inverter-based microwave or a fan-heavy toaster oven hybrid.

### 2. Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($150-$500)
The price for these units varies significantly based on technology and capacity. Budget models typically fall between $150 and $200 and offer basic air frying and microwave functions. Mid-range units from $250 to $350 often include inverter technology and smart sensors. High-end premium models with smart home integration, large capacities, and specialty modes like rotisserie or yogurt making can range from $400 to $500.

### 3. Key Features - Most important technical specifications
Look closely at the wattage and the turntable size. A minimum of 900 watts is recommended for the microwave function to ensure fast reheating. For the air fry and convection functions, look for units with at least 1500 watts of power to achieve that desired crispiness. Inverter technology is also a huge plus as it allows for constant, even power rather than the standard on-and-off pulse method.

### 4. Quality & Durability - What to look for in construction
A stainless steel interior is the gold standard for these combo units. It is much more durable than painted metal and far easier to clean after a greasy air-frying session. Check the weight of the unit as well; a heavier machine often indicates better insulation and higher-quality internal components. Sturdy door hinges and responsive buttons are also signs of a well-constructed appliance.

### 5. Brand Reputation - Importance of established manufacturers
