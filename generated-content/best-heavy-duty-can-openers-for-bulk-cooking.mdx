## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Heavy Duty Can Openers For Bulk Cooking

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before making a purchase, identify the specific types of cans you will be opening most frequently. If your bulk cooking involves #10 industrial-sized cans, you must choose an opener with a long crank and a heavy-duty frame. Smaller handheld models are great for standard soup or bean cans, but they may flex or jam when faced with larger, thicker metal lids.
