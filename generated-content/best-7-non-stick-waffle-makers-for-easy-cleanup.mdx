## What to Look for Before Buying the Best 7 Non Stick Waffle Makers For Easy Cleanup

Selecting the right waffle maker involves more than just picking the one that looks the best. You need to consider how it fits into your daily routine and your kitchen space.

### 1. Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Think about how many people you usually feed. If you are cooking for a large family, look for high-wattage models like the Novalife, which cook faster. If you are an apartment dweller, a vertical-storing model like the Bella Flip-n-Store will be your best friend. Also, consider the style of waffle you prefer; Belgian waffles are thick and deep-pocketed, while classic waffles are thinner and crispier.

### 2. Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($30-$80 ranges)
Waffle makers vary significantly in price. You can find basic, reliable models in the $30 to $40 range that do an excellent job. However, if you want premium features like removable plates, adjustable browning, or high-end ceramic coatings, expect to spend between $50 and $80. Investing a bit more upfront often results in a more durable machine that is easier to clean.

### 3. Key Features - Most important technical specifications
