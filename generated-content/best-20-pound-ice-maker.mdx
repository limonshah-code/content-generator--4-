## What to Look for Before Buying the Best 20 Pound Ice Maker

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before you purchase, determine if you need a portable countertop unit or a permanent commercial installation. Countertop models are great for occasional use or camping as they require no plumbing. Commercial units, however, offer much higher production rates and larger storage bins, making them essential for high-demand areas like restaurants or large families.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($100-$500 ranges)
Portable models typically fall in the $100 to $200 range and are budget-friendly options for basic ice needs. Commercial-style units with 20-pound storage bins usually start around $300 and can go up to $600 depending on features like nugget ice production or built-in freezer functions. Investing more usually gets you better insulation and faster cycle times.

### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
