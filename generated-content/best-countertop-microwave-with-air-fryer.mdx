## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Countertop Microwave With Air Fryer

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before making a purchase, assess your typical cooking habits. If you primarily reheat leftovers but want the occasional crispy fries, a smaller 0.9 cubic foot model is likely sufficient. However, if you plan to use the convection and air fry modes for full family meals or roasting whole chickens, prioritize units with at least 1.3 to 1.5 cubic feet of space. Consider whether you need specialized functions like yogurt making or rotisserie as these are not standard in all hybrid models.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($150-$400 ranges)
Countertop microwave air fryer combos generally fall into three price tiers.
