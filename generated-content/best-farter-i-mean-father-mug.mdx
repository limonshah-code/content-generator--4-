## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Farter I Mean Father Mug

### 1. Find Your Need
The first step is deciding between ceramic and stainless steel.
Ceramic is traditional, feels great for home use, and is usually microwave safe.
Stainless steel tumblers are better for dads who travel, commute, or spend time outdoors, as they provide insulation and are more durable.

### 2. Budget
Novelty mugs typically range from $12 to $30.
Basic ceramic mugs are usually at the lower end ($12-$18), while larger 15oz mugs or insulated stainless steel tumblers fall in the $20-$30 range.
Consider if the extra cost for insulation or a gift box is worth it for your specific situation.

### 3. Key Features
Look for mugs that are dishwasher and microwave safe to ensure maximum convenience.
The quality of the print is also vital; look for sublimation printing which fuses the ink into the material to prevent peeling.
If buying a tumbler, check for a BPA-free, spill-resistant lid.

### 4. Quality & Durability
High-quality ceramic should be thick-walled and lead-free.
For stainless steel, 18/8 food-grade steel is the industry standard for safety and durability.
Check user reviews for mentions of chip resistance and how well the print holds up after multiple washes.

### 5. Brand Reputation
While many novelty items come from smaller brands, look for those with a history of positive feedback regarding shipping safety and print quality.
Brands that offer money-back guarantees or responsive customer service provide extra peace of mind.

### 6. Portability/Size
Consider where your dad will use the mug most.
An 11oz mug is standard for a quick cup of coffee, while a 15oz or 20oz vessel is better for those who don't want to keep refilling.
Ensure that travel tumblers have a base that fits standard car cup holders if he plans to use it on the go.
