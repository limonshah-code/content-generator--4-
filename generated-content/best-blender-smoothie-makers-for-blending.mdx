## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Blender Smoothie Makers For Blending

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before you purchase a blender, consider what you will be making most often. If your daily routine consists of simple protein shakes with soft fruits, a basic 700-watt model like the Ninja Fit is more than sufficient. However, if you are a fan of thick acai bowls or need to grind nuts into butter, you should look for models with a built-in tamper or higher peak wattage, like the Ninja Foodi SS101.
