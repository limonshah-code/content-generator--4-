## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Double Xl Air Fryer

### 1. Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before you choose a model, consider how you typically cook. Do you need to air fry two different foods like wings and fries at the same time? If so, a dual-basket model is essential. If you prefer baking sheet pan meals or roasting large chickens, a countertop oven style might serve you better. Evaluate your family size to ensure the quart capacity meets your daily requirements.

### 2. Budget - Setting realistic price expectations
Air fryers in the double XL category usually range from 130 to 300 dollars. Entry-level dual-basket models are often priced lower, while those with integrated thermometers and professional oven features sit at the higher end. Setting a budget helps you decide if smart features like temperature probes are a must-have or a luxury you can skip.

### 3. Key Features - Most important technical specifications
