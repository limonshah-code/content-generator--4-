## What to Look for Before Buying the Best 3 Handset Cordless Phones With Answering Machines

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before you purchase a system, think about how you plan to use it. Do you need a phone in every room, or just in the main living areas? If you have a multi-story home, you might want to look for models with an intercom feature so you can communicate between handsets without shouting. Compatibility with your existing landline provider or VoIP service is usually guaranteed with DECT 6.0 technology, but always double-check if you have specific digital requirements.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($X-$Y ranges)
A standard 3-handset system usually ranges from $60 to $120. Entry-level models around $60 typically offer basic answering machines and caller ID. Mid-range options ($80-$100) often include advanced call blocking and larger displays. Premium systems ($110+) frequently offer Bluetooth 'Connect to Cell' features and higher-quality finishes like metallic black or champagne.

### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
Look for DECT 6.0 technology, as it provides the most secure and interference-free connection. Answering machine capacity is another big one; aim for at least 14 minutes, though 22 minutes is better for busy households. Full-duplex speakerphones are a must if you plan on doing a lot of hands-free talking, as they prevent the audio from cutting out when both people speak.

### Quality & Durability - What to look for in construction
Durability in cordless phones is often found in the keypad and the battery life. Look for models with 'soft-touch' buttons that are rated for millions of presses.
