## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Balcony Privacy Screens Under 20

Choosing the right privacy screen involves more than just picking a color. You need to ensure the product matches your specific railing type and environmental conditions. Here are the key factors to consider before making your purchase.

### 1. Find Your Need - Compatibility and Use Case
Before buying, evaluate your balcony railing. Is it metal, wood, or a chain link fence? Most screens under 20 are designed for versatility, but some include better attachment options for specific structures. If you have a very long railing, looking for a 19 or 20-foot screen is more efficient than piecing together several 10-foot ones. Consider if you need the screen primarily for privacy, wind reduction, or to keep a pet safe from falling.

### 2. Budget - Setting Realistic Price Expectations
In the under 20 range, you can expect high-quality HDPE materials and basic zip-tie installation kits. While you won't get motorized retractable features, you can certainly find heavy-duty knitted fabrics and rust-resistant grommets. Spending closer to 20 often gets you reinforced edges and better UV protection, while the lower end of the budget might offer thinner mesh suitable for temporary use or low-wind areas.

### 3. Key Features - Technical Specifications
Look for the GSM (Grams per Square Meter) rating. A higher GSM (like 175 or 185) indicates a denser, more durable fabric that provides better privacy. UV protection is also critical; aim for products offering 90 percent or higher to ensure the screen doesn't become brittle after one summer. Grommet material matters too—brass and aluminum are the gold standards for resisting rust in rain and snow.

### 4. Quality & Durability - Construction Details
Examine the edges of the screen. The best options feature double-stitched hemming or reinforced binding. This prevents the grommets from pulling out when the wind catches the screen like a sail. Also, prioritize 'new' HDPE over recycled materials, as new HDPE is generally stronger and more resistant to the elements over a 3 to 5-year period.

### 5. Brand Reputation - Established Manufacturers
Brands like Windscreen4less, SUNNY GUARD, and Junkogo have established track records in the outdoor shading market. ## What Is the Best Way to Choose balcony privacy screens under 20?

The best way to choose the perfect screen is to follow a systematic approach. Start by measuring the total linear feet of your railing and its height. Do not guess; an extra 6 inches can make the installation look messy. Once you have your dimensions, decide on the color. Black and dark grey offer the best visibility blocking, while tan or green might blend better with your surroundings.

Next, look for products that specifically mention reinforced edges and a high UV block rate. If you live in a windy area, prioritize screens with closer grommet spacing (every 12 to 18 inches) to ensure you can tie it down securely. Finally, check what is included in the package. A screen that comes with 50 to 100 zip ties saves you an extra trip to the hardware store and ensures you have enough to secure every single grommet.
