## What to Look for Before Buying the Best 40 Gallon Propane Water Heaters

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before you purchase, determine how many fixtures you need to run at the same time. While a 40-gallon tank can run out of water, a tankless unit is measured by its Gallons Per Minute (GPM). If you have a two-bathroom home, aim for a unit with at least 4.0 to 5.0 GPM. If you live in a colder climate where the incoming water is very chilly, you will need a higher BTU rating to ensure the heater can raise the temperature sufficiently.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($300-$1,200 ranges)
Propane water heaters vary widely in price. Budget-friendly models typically range from $300 to $500 and are perfect for smaller homes or apartments. Mid-range units with better smart controls and higher GPM ratings fall between $500 and $800. ### Key Features - Most important technical specifications

## What Is the Best Way to Choose 40 gallon propane water heaters?

Choosing the right unit starts with a professional assessment of your gas line capacity and venting options. You should first measure the incoming water temperature during the coldest month of the year. This 'groundwater temperature' determines how much 'rise' your heater needs to provide. For example, if your groundwater is 50 degrees and you want 120-degree showers, your heater needs to provide a 70-degree rise. Check the GPM chart for any heater you consider to see how many gallons it can produce at that specific temperature rise.

Once you know your GPM requirement, decide between indoor and outdoor installation. Outdoor units are easier to install as they do not require venting through the roof, but they are not suitable for extremely cold climates where the pipes could freeze. Next, look for safety features like flame failure protection and anti-freeze sensors. Finally, always compare the warranty terms; a longer warranty on the heat exchanger is usually a good indicator of the manufacturer's confidence in their product quality.
